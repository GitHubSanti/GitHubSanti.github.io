About Me

Hi, I am Santi. I was born in Medellin, Colombia and moved to the US at the age of six. I was raised in Charlotte, North Carolina and went to college at NC State for Biomedical Engineering. This was where I was first introduced to coding with a microcontrollers class where we coded first in asembly language and then in C. With my first job at Oracle I got a chance to learn SQL thru my job role and used company resources to become Java certified. When I changed jobs and started working for Fujifilm Medical Systems, I enrolled in a fullstack web development course to learn about CSS, HTML, javascript, jQuery, Bootstrap, Node.js, React.js and a variety of other technologies.

I also have other interests besides coding. I am a sports fan, specifically basketball and football professional or collegiate.
